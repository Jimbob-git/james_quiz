5 Facts about Singapore

1. Singapore's land mass is not just one island but is actually made up of 64 islands.

2. The Merlion is the symbol of Singapore, it is half lion and half fish. It has the head of a lion and the body of a fish.

3. There are only 3 city-states in the World (A city that is a country), of which Singapore is one. The other two are San Marino and the Vatican City.

4. Singapore's Changi Airport is the World's best airport 2022. This is an award it also won from 2013 to 2020, 7 years in a row. It features a trampoline, a giant slide, indoor forest, the tallest indoor waterfall in the World, a cinema, a swimming pool and hotels where you can rent a bed by the hour!

5. There are no seasons in Singapore, it has a tropical rainforest climate. It lies within 100 miles of the equator and always has a temperature of between 25 to 35 degrees celsius or 77 to 95 degrees fahrenheit
