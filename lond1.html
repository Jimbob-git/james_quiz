5 Facts about London

1. London went through a period called the 'Gin Craze' between 1720 and 1751. During the height of the Gin Craze, the average weekly consumtpion of gin stood at 2 pints per person for every person in the city, including children!

2. Every year since 1947 the country of Norway ships a large Christmas tree to the city of London, which is displayed in Trafalgar Square as a thank you for the Uk's alliance during world war II.

3. During World War II, London functioned as the capital of six countries that had been invaded. The countries were Belgium, France, Holland, Norway, Poland and of course the United Kingdom

4. Back in the 18th century, one could gain access into London Zoo free of charge, if you brought a dog or cat to feed to the lions!

5. In 1878, London University was the first University in the United Kingdom to grant women permission to study as well as award them Degrees.
