5 Facts about Auckland

1. Auckland used to be the Capital of New Zealand between 1842 and 1865 before Wellington took the title.

2. Tamaki Makaurau is the Maori name for Auckland, which translates to 'Tamaki with a hundred lovers'.

3. There are over 50 extinct volcanoes in the city of Auckland, which derive from a magma source located 100 kilometers under the city

4. The City of Sails is a nickname by which Auckland is known. There are over half a million boats moored in marinas around the city.

5. Auckland is home to the World's largest Polynesian population, making up 28% of Auckland's total population
