5 Facts about Mumbai

1. For over 300 years the city of Mumbai was known as Bombay. In 1995, the ruling political party, 'Shev Sena', changed the name to Mumbai

2. As of 2022, Mumbai is the most populated city in India, with a population of over 20 million residents

3. On the 16th April, 1853, India's first train commenced. Nowadays, The Mumbai local train network is the World's busiest railway system, carrying 2.2 billion passengers each year

4. Mumbai is home to the World's best managed supply chain network, called the 'Dabbawallas'.
