5 Facts about Rarotonga

1. Rarotonga is only 32 kilometers in circumference, which means that you can get to anywhere on the island within 20 minutes

2. There is no such thing is a high rise building on Rarotonga, as they have a law preventing any new construction being taller than a coconut tree!

3. Rarotonga has a high peak in the middle of the island called Te Manga, it reaches 653m above sea level and offers spectacular views, it can be reached by trekking 3 hours up the peak but it is well worth the pain, I know, I've done it!

4. Although the official language of the Cook Islands is English, many of the local population of Rarotonga speak Cook Islands Maori, otherwise known as Rarotongan, similar to New Zealand Maori

5. Rarotonga is one of the few places in the World where you are encouraged to smile for your driving licence photo!
