5 Facts about Fiji

1. Fiji is made up of over 330 islands with over 220 of them being uninhabitated

2. If you visit a traditional Fijian village, never wear a hat and sunglasses, this is reserved for the village chief only!

3. Cannibalism was common in Fiji up until 1871, when King Ratu Cakobau ended this by telling his people to stop eating other people!

4. Fiji has 3 official languages, Fijian, English and Fiji Hindi. The Fijian language has over 200 different dialects.

5. The most important sport in Fiji is rugby, with one of their major highlights being the winning of the gold medal at the 2016 summer olympics
